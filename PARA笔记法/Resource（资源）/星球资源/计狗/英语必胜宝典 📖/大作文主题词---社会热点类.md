
# 大作文主题词---社会热点类

生理健康：physical health

心理健康：mental / psychological health

**健康问题：health concern**

亚健康：sub-health

公共卫生：public health / hygiene

心态：state of mind

**体育锻炼：physical exercise**

均衡饮食：well-balanced diet

垃圾食品：junk food

**提供医疗服务：provide medical services**

**牺牲健康：sacrifice health**

传染病：infectious disease

慢性病：chronic disease

**代沟：generation gap**

**弥合差距：bridge the gap**

相互理解：mutual understanding

误解：misunderstanding

照顾：care，take care of，look after

**老龄化社会：aging society**

**人口老龄化：population aging**

**老年人：**old / aged people，**the old / aged** /elders / elderly，**senior citizens**

奉行孝道：practice filial piety

**公德：public morality**

**传统美德：traditional virtues**

**责任：duty，responsibility**

**责任感：sense of responsibility**

**承担责任：shoulder / assume / undertake one's duties / responsibilities**

**逃避责任：avoid / shirk / shun one's duties /responsibilities**

**奉献：devote / devotion，dedicate /dedication**

**无私奉献：selfless devotion**

**信任危机：the crisis of trust**

**公德堕落：the corruption of public morality**

**道德缺失：the lack of moral sense**

**不道德行为：immoral behaviors**

**希望工程：Project Hope**

**中国红十字会：China Red Cross Foundation**

**慈善：charity**

**捐款：donate / donation**

**贫困乡村地区：poverty-stricken rural areas**

**入学：start schooling**

**辍学：drop out of school**

**完成学业：finish school**

**尊老爱幼：to respect the old and care for theyoung**

**随地吐痰：spit everywhere**

**大声喧哗：make noise，talk loudly**

**青少年犯罪：youthful offender, juveniledelinquency**

**开明的社会：a progressive / enlightened society**

**两性平等：gender equality**

**性别歧视：gender discrimination, sexism**

**机会均等：equality of opportunity**

**养育孩子：child rearing**

**家庭暴力：domestic violence**

**触犯法律：break / violate / disobey the law**

**犯罪：commit a crime**

**执法机构：law enforcement agencies**

**刑事责任：criminal liability / responsibility**

**遵守法律：abide by the law**

**富有启发的：enlightening**

**引起了警惕和重视：cause alarm and attention**

**为...树立榜样：set a good example for**

**确保实施活动：ensure implementing activities**

**易受伤害的：vulnerable**

**职业妇女：career women**

**溺爱孩子：spoil children = dote on children**

**纵容溺爱：over-indulgence**

**小康之家：a well-off family**

城市化：urbanization

人口规模膨胀：population expansion

单亲家庭: a single-parent family

独生子女：only child

全面二孩政策：the universal two-child policy

**偶像: idol**

**对……盲目/非理性的崇拜: blind/irrational worship of...**

**公交专用车道: bus lane**

(机动车）单双号限行: odd-and-even license plate rule

**早晚高峰: morning and evening peak**

**交通拥堵: traffic jam/congestion，heavy traffic**

**公共交通线路网: public transport network**

**私家车：private car**

**发展公共交通：develop public transport**

酒驾：driving under the influence (DUD)，drunk driving

行人：pedestrian

**外卖：takeout**

**共享单车：shared bikes**

**移动支付: mobile payment**
