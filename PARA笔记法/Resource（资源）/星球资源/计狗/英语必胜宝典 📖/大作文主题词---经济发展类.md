**经济危机：economic crisis , financial crissis** , financial turmoil , financial meltdown

<br>

**经济不景气： recession** , bleeding economy , depression , sluggish economy

<br>

**失业：lose one's job，be unemployed**，be redundant，unemployment

<br>

**失业人数：unemployment**

<br>

**找工作：seek employment**

<br>

**找工作者：job seeker/hunter**

<br>

**严峻的就业形势：a tough job market**

**<br>**

**经济复苏：economic recovery**

<br>

**应对危机：face the crisis **, meet the crisis , fight the crisis

<br>

**减缓衰退：slow down declines**

<br>

创造新的就业：generate new employment（国家该怎么做？用建议句）


刺激消费：stimulate consumer spending（国家该怎么做？）

<br>

扩大内需：propel/drive/expand domestic demand（当经济增长出现疲软或面临下行风险时）

（这俩记一个遇到合适情况稍微装一哈就行。。。）

适度宽松的货币政策：moderately loose monetary policy（当经济增长出现疲软或面临下行风险时）

<br>

政府紧急援助government bailout

<br>

社会保障体系：social security system

<br>

基础设施建设：infrastructure construction


**恢复：revive，recovery**，turnaround



减少裁员：refrain from laying off employees（各企业单位该怎么做？）


减员：cut the salaried workforce

<br>

经济刺激方案：stimulus package，stimulus plan

<br>

**就业指导：career guidance**

<br>

市场经济体制：the market economy system

<br>

**经济全球化：economic globalization**

<br>

国际经济组织：international economic organization

<br>

科技进步：advancements in science and technology（探讨经济和科技发展相关主题时）

<br>

跨国公司：multinational

<br>

私营企业：private enterprise

<br>

汽车产业：automobile industry（已经考过了）

<br>

**房地产业：real estate industry（可能给你一个柱状图，让你分析为什么早年房价疯涨，现在开始下跌，或者让你分析一线、二线及其他线城市房价）**

<br>

住房问题：housing problem

<br>

电信产业：telecommunication industry

<br>

国产品牌：domestic/national/Chinese brand

<br>

**市场份额：market share/portion**

<br>

**企业：enterprise**

<br>

**员工：staff，personnel**

<br>

工作满意度：job satisfaction

<br>

工作压力：work/working pressure

<br>

**提高福利待遇：improve welfare and treatment**

<br>

**吸引外资：attract foreign investment**

<br>

法律保护：legal protection

<br>

深远的社会影响：profound social and economic impact


**改革开放：the reform and opening-up【例句：****"In the context of economic reform and opening up."****】**

<br>

可持续的城市发展：sustainable urban development

<br>

**城市化：urbanization**

<br>

品牌效应：brand effect

<br>

售后服务：after-sale service

<br>

**超前消费：over-consuming**

<br>

**提升居民生活水平：enhance citizens' standard of living**

<br>

<strong>追求高薪工作：pursue a high-paid job

</strong>

**没有或缺乏实践经验实践能力：the lack of practical**

<br>

职业技能：experience/hands-on

<br>

**制造商：manufacturer**

<br>

**国内市场：the domestic market**

<br>

**城市的：urban **

<br>

**农村的：rural**

<br>

**城市化：urbanization**

<br>

**人口规模膨胀：population expansion**

<br>

**医疗：medical care**

<br>

**缩小城乡差距：shorten/narrow the gap between**

<br>

**缩小贫富差距: close/narrow the gap between rich**

<br>

**减少社会矛盾：reduce social contradictions**

<br>

**生活节奏加快：The tempo/pace of life has accelerated**

<br>

**不正当竞争: unfair competition**

<br>

**繁荣: boom**

<br>

**信用危机: credit crisis**

<br>

**餐厅: canteen**

<br>

**创业开公司: start one's own business**

<br>

**满意: satisfaction**

<br>

**移动支付: mobile payment**

<br>

**共享单车: shared bikes **

<br>

**高铁: high-speed trains**

<br>

**网络购物: online shopping**

<br>

**经济不景气: recession/bleeding economy**

<br>

**经济金融危机: economic/financial crisis**

<br>

**计划生育政策: the family planning policy/ the one-child policy**

<br>

**刺激经济发展: spur/stimulate economic development**

<br>
