
# 完型词汇总结---（结合易熙人和宋逸轩老师）

### **完型****相同****逻辑关系词**

<br>

总分关系：for example(加逗号可作为插入语) / for instance, such as, **including**...

因果关系：

      原因词：because,** in that**, **since**, as, **for（表原因不能放在句首，只能放后面，for表推理，例句：The days are getting hoter and hoter,**** for**** summer comes）**, as a result of, **considering**

结果词：so that, such that, as a result, lead to,** consequently**, therefor, hence, thus, so

并列关系：and, or, as well as, like, likewise, similarly, equally, **simultaneously**, **meanwhile**, [either...or...](http://either...or...), [neither...nor...](http://neither...nor...), that is to say...

<br>

### 完型相反逻辑关系词

<br>

对立关系：**while(皇后词)**, whereas, unlike, **against(与...相对)**, instead of, **rather than**, contray to, on the contrary, by contrast, conversely, as, **[not...but...](http://not...but...)(不是...就是...)**

转折关系：but, **however(皇后词，也可以表让步！！！)**, yet, unfortunately, **otherwise,** nevertheless

让步关系：although, though, even though, even, **despite**, in spite of, even if, **however(表让步时，译为无论如何，不管怎样), ****now that(引导让步状语从句，译为既然)**

<br>

### 皇后词

<br>

**while：①：虽然，表让步（句首） ②：然而，表对比（句中）**

**despite：尽管，表让步**

**even：甚至，表递进**

**for example/ for instance/ such as/ including：**表演绎（举例）

**otherwise：否则，表转折**

**according to.../ in terms of...：根据...**

**in fact：暗含转折**

because/** in that：**表因果

**however**/but：表转折

**as：**像/由于，**表因果（也可以表让步**，译为尽管，但考的少）

then：表顺承

**against：表对立**

**subject：被试=实验对象**

turn：轮流/依次，表明次序上的前后相接

**mark：v.评分，做记号; n.记号，目标**

**expose：****使****遭受**（If someone** is exposed to **something dangerous or unpleasant, they are put in a situation in which it might affect them.）

** divide:分开，分割，除以**

**raise：引起，养育，增加**

particular

tend+tendency:趋势

**account：**n.账户，账目，**叙述；v.说明，占...；accountable：负责任的**

sharp灵敏的+sharpness清晰度

sustain：保持

maintain：保持，维持，坚持认为...

obtain：获得

retain：保留

attain：达到，获得

suggest：建议，**暗示**

<br>
