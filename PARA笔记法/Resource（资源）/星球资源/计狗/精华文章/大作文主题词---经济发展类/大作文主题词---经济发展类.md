<strong>恢复：revive，recovery</strong>，turnaround

<strong>改革开放：the reform and opening-up【例句：</strong><strong style="background-color: rgb(20, 23, 24); color: rgb(254, 254, 254);">"In the context of economic reform and opening up."</strong><strong>】</strong></p><p><br></p><p>可持续的城市发展：sustainable urban development</p><p><br></p><p><strong style="color: rgb(236, 40, 52);">城市化：urbanization</strong></p><p><br></p><p>品牌效应：brand effect</p><p><br></p><p>售后服务：after-sale service</p><p><br></p><p><strong>超前消费：over-consuming</strong></p><p><br></p><p><strong>提升居民生活水平：enhance citizens' standard of living</strong></p><p><br></p><p><strong>追求高薪工作：pursue a high-paid job
